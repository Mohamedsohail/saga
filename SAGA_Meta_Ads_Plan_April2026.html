Lead Quality Filter: The "buying timeline" question in Form A and "budget range" in Form B act as natural quality filters. Leads selecting "Just exploring" or budgets under ₹20L can be deprioritized in follow-up. This keeps CPL in range while improving lead-to-visit conversion.

Optimization Playbook

Daily, weekly, and trigger-based optimization rules
📅 Daily Optimization Checklist
☐ Check CPL by ad set — flag any above ₹400
☐ Review frequency — pause ad sets above 3.0
☐ Check lead quality from CRM/form responses
☐ Monitor spend pacing — ensure budget is being used
☐ Review any ad rejections or policy flags
☐ Check form completion rate — target above 12%
📊 Weekly Optimization Actions
☐ Kill ads with CTR below 1.5% after 1,000 impressions
☐ Kill ad sets with CPL above ₹400 after 5+ leads
☐ Scale winning ad sets by 25-30% budget increase
☐ Launch 2-3 new creatives in testing campaign
☐ Move proven winners from Test → Core campaign
☐ Refresh retargeting creatives every 2 weeks
☐ Analyze lead-to-visit conversion by ad set
🔄 Trigger-Based Rules
Trigger Condition Action
CPL Spike CPL > ₹400 for 3 consecutive days Pause ad set → Check creative fatigue → Launch fresh creative
Low CTR CTR < 1.0% after 2,000 impressions Pause ad → Replace with new hook/angle
Winner Found CPL < ₹280 with 10+ leads Duplicate to Core campaign → Increase budget 30%
High Frequency Frequency > 3.5 in any ad set Expand audience OR pause and refresh creative
Junk Leads Lead quality < 25% on any ad set Switch to Higher Intent form → Tighten targeting
Budget Unspent Less than 80% daily budget spent Loosen cost cap → Expand audience → Add placements
