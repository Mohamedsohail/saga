🤖 Why Advantage+ Broad?
Meta's algorithm in 2026 is extremely powerful at finding converters without manual interest stacking. By giving it a broad audience with strong creative signals, it finds buyers we'd never think to target manually. Often delivers the lowest CPL in real estate campaigns.
How it works:
→ Meta uses your pixel data + form submission patterns
→ Algorithm finds "lookalike behaviors" automatically
→ Reaches people who BEHAVE like buyers but don't match interest categories
→ Requires strong creative (video with clear property + price)

⚠️ Prerequisite: Fix lead tracking first. Without conversion data, Advantage+ has nothing to optimize toward.
